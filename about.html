About Us

Pine City Mall is your premier retail destination in the Western Cape province. It is the largest and most popular retail destination across the Cape Town area. Pine City Mall offers a unique retail experience by fusing inspiration with international style through it's assorted mix of fashion,food and leisure,attracting millions of shoppers each year.

Pine City Mall opened on the 8th June 2016, the expansion has further strengthened Pine City Mall's position in Cape Town.
